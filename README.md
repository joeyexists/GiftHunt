# GiftHunt

A scuffed interpretation of Luigi's Balloon World in Neon White.

### Hiding Gifts

* Press the hide gift hotkey (default: `Right Shift`) to hide a gift where you're standing. A seed will be copied to your clipboard, which includes the level, position, and your dev time.

> The seed will also appear in the MelonLoader console, or you can find it in `Neon White\MelonLoader\Latest.log`

### Finding Gifts

* To load a gift, copy a gift seed and press the load gift hotkey (default: `\`) or paste it into the gift seed field within melon preferences. The mod will automatically load the level and the gift.

### Other Features

* **Dev Times** - Every gift seed records your hide time, and other players can see it after beating it.
* **Custom Keybinds** - All keybinds can be changed in melon preferences.
* **PB Tracking** - Your PB is tracked while a gift seed is loaded, with green numbers included!
* **Does not affect your gift count** - Collecting GiftHunt gifts wont increment your gift count, so you don't have to worry about getting stuck in an endless loop giving hundreds of gifts ðŸ”¥

## Installation

1. Install [Melonloader v0.6.1](https://github.com/LavaGang/MelonLoader/releases/tag/v0.6.1) onto your Neon White game.
2. Make sure you have [Melon Preferences Manager](https://github.com/Bluscream/MelonPreferencesManager) and [UniverseLib](https://github.com/sinai-dev/UniverseLib) installed (included in the [NeonLite](https://github.com/Faustas156/NeonLite) setup).
3. Download [the latest GiftHunt release](https://github.com/joeyexists/GiftHunt/releases/latest) and drop the `GiftHunt.dll` into your `Neon White\Mods` folder.
4. Press `F5` in-game to open melon preferences and change the mod settings.
